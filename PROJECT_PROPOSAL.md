## 2. Core Features & Capabilities
Building on a foundation of performance-first architecture, the application will provide:

*   **Intelligent Student Registry:** Comprehensive profiles including academic history, medical data, and caregiver associations.
*   **Compliance & Discipline Engine:** Automated tracking of parent-teacher meetings and bill submissions, with real-time "Warning" and "Good" status auto-calculation.
*   **Welfare Distribution Tracker:** Precision management of product distributions, expenditure reporting, and donor tracking.
*   **Masterpiece Dashboard:** A high-end visual command center for administrators, offering instant insights into grade distribution, gender statistics, and compliance alerts.
*   **PDF Reporting Suite:** Automated generation of academic and welfare reports.
